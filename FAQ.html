Membership & Pricing

We offer several membership tiers to fit your needs:
  • Basic: Gym access during standard hours
  • Premium: 24/7 access + group classes
  • Elite: All Premium benefits + personal training sessions
  • Student/Senior: Discounted rates with valid ID
All memberships include a free fitness assessment and orientation.

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons, travel, or other personal circumstances. A small monthly maintenance fee applies during the freeze period. Please contact our front desk at least one week before your next billing cycle to arrange a freeze.

  • Family Plans: 15% discount for additional family members

Facility & Equipment

Our standard operating hours are:
  • Monday - Friday: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Saturday: 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Premium and Elite members have 24/7 access using their key fob. Holiday hours may vary.

Our facility includes:
  • Cardio zone with treadmills, ellipticals, and bikes
  • Extensive free weights area
  • Strength training machines
  • Functional training area with TRX, kettlebells, and battle ropes
  • Group exercise studio
  • Locker rooms with showers and sauna
  • Sports nutrition bar
  • Basketball court

Training & Classes

  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
  • Yoga (Vinyasa, Hatha, and Restorative)
  • Spin/Cycling
  • Pilates
  • Zumba
  • Bootcamp
  • Strength Training
  • Boxing & MMA
Class schedules are updated monthly and available on our app and website.
