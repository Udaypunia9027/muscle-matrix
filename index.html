Whey (protein)

Whey protein is a high-quality protein derived from milk during the cheese-making process. It is considered a complete protein as it contains all nine essential amino acids that the body cannot produce on its own. It is rapidly absorbed by the body, making it ideal for consumption after a workout to kickstart muscle repair and growth. Additionally, it can aid in promoting satiety, which helps in weight management goals.
